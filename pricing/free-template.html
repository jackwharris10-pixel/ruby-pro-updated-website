How we built the template

We collected price grids from more than twenty-five distributors: large and small, single-state and multi-state, legacy systems and modern ERPs.

We looked at:

  • How they convert cases, bottles, liters, and gallons
  • How FOB, landed cost, and distributor margin are shown on one line
  • How depletion allowances and supplier programs are layered in
  • How freight, taxes, and fees are tracked without breaking the math
  • How sales teams see pricing and how finance teams audit it

Then we removed the noise, kept the patterns that work, and turned it into a single template any supplier can use to “speak distributor” from day one.
