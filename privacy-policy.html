Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Who We Are

Ruby Pro Software provides ERP and pricing management software for wine and spirits distributors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website at rubyprosoftware.com.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email, phone number, and message when you submit our contact form
  • Business information when you request a demo or download resources

Information collected automatically:

  • Page views, session duration, and navigation patterns via Google Analytics (GA4)
  • Company and professional information via Apollo.io website tracking for business development purposes
  • Browser type, device, and IP address

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services on this website:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — Website analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google may collect data according to their own privacy policy.
  • Apollo.io — Business intelligence and lead enrichment. Apollo may identify your company based on your visit to provide us with business contact information.
  • Google reCAPTCHA v2 — Spam protection on our contact form. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • BSS Smart Forms — Contact form processing and delivery.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and demo requests
  • To understand how visitors use our website and improve it
  • To identify potential business customers for our software products
  • To send relevant follow-up communications about our products

Cookies

Our website uses cookies from Google Analytics and Apollo.io to track website usage and identify business visitors. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the third-party services listed above to operate our website and business development activities.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. Contact us at sales@rubyprosoftware.com with any privacy-related requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at sales@rubyprosoftware.com.
